The Magic of Scale-to-Zero

The single most important cost-saving technique is deploying with a minimum replica count of zero. This means you only pay for compute when the endpoint is actively processing a request.

Cold Start: The first request after a period of inactivity takes longer (e.g., 20-40s) because Vertex AI must provision a server from scratch. This is the peak on the chart.

Warm Instance: Subsequent requests are very fast (e.g., 1-3s) because the server is now running and "warm." This is the flat line on the chart. For apps needing instant response, a minimum of 1 replica is kept running 24/7 at a higher cost.
